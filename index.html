Conquer Rome I

  • High Concept: Defeat the Ceasar and Conquer Rome!
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should feel motivated to destroy the remaining Romans.
  • Genre: RPG
  • Game play: Players control a barbarian during the seige of Rome. Use his axe to cut down the remaining Romans and kill the Ceasar to Conquer Rome.
  • Prototype goal: Having fun on tilemaps.
  • Features: Different levels of complex tilemaps with enemy soldiers and items on them.
  • Setting/Premise: The Barbarians are marching against Rome, and the Caesar must die. Fight through the waves of the weak and foolish Romans to make it to the royal palace.
  • Story: For years Rome forced their beliefs on the germanic tribes in an attempt to control them. The Roman way shall be no more. The tribes have banded together in an attempt to push back the weaking power of Rome and take back what is ours. Fight the Romans, take the City, and kill the Ceasar.
  • Target Audience: This game is intended to edjucate about the fall of Rome, and is also for entertainment so it is open to all audiences. Specifically this game focuses on the population between the ages 12 and 28 since it is very simple and has conflict in it.
  • Play time: The game can be played for as long as the player wishes.
  • Strategies: Kill all the Romans without dying. Step back and take a rest if you need to heal.
  • Prototype Assets: I got my original spritesheets off of google and edited them in gimp to better fit my game.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: I borrowed help from the top down rpg devgameacademy example found in the readme file.
